Mission & Mandate

The Identity

Born from the discipline of the 10th Special Forces Group and committed to the legacy of our European Christian heritage, we serve as a digital vanguard. We do not create merely for entertainment; we create for posterity. We are the unseen force in a media landscape that has abandoned the truth—we are the ghost in the machine, haunting the projects we build to ensure they carry the weight and substance of our values.

The Foundation

We operate under the absolute authority of the Triune God—the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit—the one and only true God. We hold the 1560 Geneva Bible as the key text of the reformation and the founding while further recognizing and acknowledging that it is only through the Holy Spirit that we are granted the discernment to properly understand and apply His truth to the world we inhabit.

The Standards

Tactical Precision: We bring the rigor of my service to every line of code, every design choice, and every narrative beat. There is no room for the “good enough” culture of a dying world. We execute with the discipline of those who know what it means to hold the line.

Stewardship of Kin: Every project is a contribution to the cultural survival of our people. We treat our work as a covert op, blending efficiency and uncompromising focus to build the parallel institutions necessary for our survival.

Unapologetic Truth: We build games and digital media that do not apologize for who we are. We provide a space for our kin to gather, to think, and to sharpen one another.

We believe the United States was established as a nation for the descendants of European Christian settlers—a land secured for our posterity to flourish and a mandate to spread the light of Christ to the world. We reject the corrosive lies of the modern era and the globalist decay that seeks to uproot our heritage. We act because weak men sold off the national birthright long ago; we are here to reclaim it.

The Objective

Whether it is through high-stakes RPGs or professional digital development, our mandate is clear: to build the bulwarks necessary for our future. We adapt, we improvise, and we deliver—quietly, efficiently, and with the conviction that our faith and bloodline remain unextinguished.

We are not just a media group; we are a fortress.

FOR THE GLORY OF GOD AND THE PRESERVATION OF OUR NATION.
